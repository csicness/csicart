About Me





My name is Curtis Chin and I'm an Artist who is based out of Providence, Rhode Island. I graduated from Rhode Island College with a B.A in Printmaking. I have a passion for all things creative. My work is made up of Illustration, Concept-Art, and Web Design.

My work is very simplistic. While working toward my degree, I focused on woodblock printing. This type of art forced me to think in a minimalistic way. This way of thinking translated into all the other type of art I do.
